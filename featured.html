Search tips

Enter your keywords in the search box and click on search. The results can be refined afterwards using the links on the left side of the results list.


You can chose between the tabs "My library" or "All libraries"

  • With My library you search only resources from the institutional view that is selected including books and electronic articles that your library has subscriptions/access to
  • With All libraries you search within the entire BIBSYS Library Catalogue and articles that your library has subscriptions/access to
  • The search result can be limited to only include resources from the library catalogue or be expanded to also include articles which your library does not have full text access to (Expand My Results)
